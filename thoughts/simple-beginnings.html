Simple Beginnings

December 2025

Everything starts somewhere. A conversation, a project, a relationship—they all begin with a single moment, a single word, a single decision.

I've been thinking about how we make things more complicated than they need to be. We plan and strategize and overthink, when often the best approach is just to start.

This website is a perfect example. Instead of spending weeks planning the perfect design, I just started with something simple. Clean, minimal, focused on content. It's not perfect, but it's real.

There's something beautiful about simple beginnings. They're honest. They don't pretend to be more than they are. They leave room to grow.

Maybe that's the key—starting simple and letting things evolve naturally. Less planning, more doing. Less complexity, more clarity.
